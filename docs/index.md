## What is GridFPV?

GridFPV is a self-hosted application for **timing and managing FPV drone races**. It keeps
track of your pilots, classes, and event structure; talks to your timing hardware (or a
built-in simulator); runs each heat through a clear lifecycle; and produces live standings
and final results.

It is designed to be **owned and operated by you** — the race director, club, or event
organizer. There is no mandatory cloud service and no account to sign up for. You run
GridFPV on your own machine and your event data lives there with you.
